I started my journey in Web Design and Development two years ago with the intent of trying to make dreams become a reality for websites. I was born into a very creative family that taught me that creativity is best done through love. Love for the hearts desire in all things beautiful. Upon graduating High School, I followed my dream of serving my country in the United States Air Force, developing skills in computers, interpersonal relationships and learning what leadership really stood for. Upon being faced with a different direction, I moved towards a degree with an amazing University. Full Sail University opened my eyes to the wide world of Web Design and Development. Through the course of my time at Full Sail University, I faced frustration, determination, desire and ultimate sacrifices to achieve what I feel is my true direction. I found that love of Web Design and Development was my true passion and desire. I wish to design and develop websites that are inspiring, natural and give people a sense of companionship within there sites. To work with people that want to bring there hopes and dreams to light. To create a picture on the screen from there imagination and bring there hearts desire for success to a reality. The Web Design process is awe inspiring and I find that I really love the initial phases of the process in working with people to get there ideas and inspirations onto paper and then further enhance there ideas into a reality. I am a mother of two wonderful children and a wife to a dedicated serviceman that has helped me throughout this direction that I have chosen to take. There love and compassion for this road we have taken together has brought a commitment to focusing on what truly matters and that is Love.
